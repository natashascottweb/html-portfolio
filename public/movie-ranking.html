The Best Movies According to Natasha Scott

My Favorite Movies of All Time

The Notebook

I consider this to be the best love movie of all time. It is also my favorite movie overall.

300

I usually don't like period movies, but 300 is full of action. There are numerous fight scenes that I consider to be iconic.

Think Like a Man

I wanted to add a comedy to this list. This is one my favorite comedy/ romance movies.
